# BKRM — Design Reference

## Inspiration

Styled after [parlor.social](https://parlor.social/):
- Clean, minimal layout with generous whitespace
- Centered sections with editorial typography
- Widely-spaced logo (B K R M)
- Pill-shaped CTA button with bold accent color
- Full-bleed imagery sections
- Horizontal scrolling gallery
- "Presented by" section dividers throughout
- Serif headings, sans-serif body text

## Three Design Variants

### Design A — Clean Mint (Parlor Direct)
- **Background**: White (#FFFFFF)
- **Accent**: Mint green (#3EDBB0)
- **Fonts**: Playfair Display (serif headings) + Inter (body)
- **Vibe**: Closest to Parlor — airy, bright, club-editorial
- **CTA**: Mint pill, black text

### Design B — Dark Gold Luxury
- **Background**: Near-black (#0A0A0A)
- **Accent**: Gold (#D4A853) with ambient glow
- **Fonts**: DM Serif Display + DM Sans
- **Vibe**: Evening lounge, private members' club
- **CTA**: Gold pill, dark text
- **Special**: Subtle radial glow behind hero, column dividers in experience grid

### Design C — Warm Earthy Editorial
- **Background**: Warm cream (#FAF7F2)
- **Accent**: Terracotta/burnt red (#B8432F)
- **Fonts**: Cormorant Garamond (serif) + Instrument Sans
- **Vibe**: High-end magazine, editorial warmth
- **CTA**: Terracotta pill, white text
- **Special**: 2-column experience cards with hover borders, dark section for Porsche partnership

## Design Principles

- Bold but restrained — premium without being flashy
- Whitespace is a feature, not empty space
- Typography carries the weight; imagery supports
- Every section breathable and scannable
- Mobile-first responsive
- No heavy frameworks — pure HTML/CSS for speed
