FROM THE FOUNDER

Proximity shapes possibility. It's a belief that has guided everything behind BackRoom.

I come from a family of individuals who chose a different path. It began with my grandmother, who left Trinidad to build a life in the United States — despite skepticism — reshaping the trajectory of our family for generations.

Her decision set in motion a legacy rooted in ambition, education, and entrepreneurship. Growing up in that environment, I saw firsthand how proximity — to opportunity, to information, to the right people — can change everything.

That legacy lives on in me.

I've always been drawn to spaces where conversations around ideas, business, and ambition are the norm — not the exception. But those spaces are rare. BackRoom exists to create them.

It's an intentional environment where founders and operators connect — not just on what they're building, but what it takes to build.

When the right people come together, something shifts — ideas move faster, conversations go deeper, and possibility expands.

This is where the people changing the world find each other.

Ashanti Callender
Founder, BackRoom
